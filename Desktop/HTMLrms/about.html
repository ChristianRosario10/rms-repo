About Rod Machine Shop

Our History

Founded in 2010, Rod Machine Shop has grown from a small local workshop to a leading machining service provider in Barangobong, Sta. Lucia. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, technological advancement, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Over the years, we've invested in state-of-the-art equipment and trained our team to deliver exceptional machining services. Our success is built on the trust of our clients and our dedication to excellence in every project we undertake.
